The Law (Companies Act 2013)

India became the first country in the world to make CSR mandatory by law. Here's why:

  • ‚úì Ensure big companies help solve social problems
  • ‚úì Make sure profits are shared with society
  • ‚úì Create a better, more equal India

Key Provisions

  • ‚Ä¢ Spend 2% of average net profit of preceding 3 years
  • ‚Ä¢ CSR Committee formation mandatory
  • ‚Ä¢ Annual CSR reporting required
  • ‚Ä¢ Penalties for non-compliance
